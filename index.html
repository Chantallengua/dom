3 stories survey

You will read 3 short stories about the history of the Dom nave that collapsed in 1674. These stories will be part of an interactive AR (Augmented Reality) app, accessible through a QR-code on Dom Square, where an entire model of the Dom Church can be placed by the user on the ground.
When you finish reading, please fill in the survey.

1. How did the nave disappear?

In 1674, a devastating storm raged over Utrecht and its surroundings. Why didn't the mighty Dom Church stand a chance against it? And was it just a storm, or was it God's wrath for the sins of its inhabitants?

2. How did the Dom Square take shape?

The ruins of the fallen nave remained for decades, becoming notorious as a meeting place for homosexual men. Why weren't the ruins cleared up? When did the Dom Square finally appear?

3. Will the nave return?

Almost 350 years after the storm, the Dom remains without a nave. Will it ever return? What would we get and what would we lose in rebuilding it?
